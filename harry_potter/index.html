Famous Wizards from Slytherin

Salazar Slytherin

One of the Four Founders of Hogwarts School For Witchcraft and Wizardry

Merlin

Former Student at Hogwarts, and a famous wizard

Phineas Nigellus Black

Great-Great Grandfather to Sirius Black and most hated former Headmaster

Tom Marvolo Riddle

Former student at Hogwarts and Lord Voldemort himself


Severus Snape

Head of Slytherin House, former Potions and Defence Against the Dark Arts Proffessor, and former Headmaster

Horace Slughorn

Retired Potions Proffessor, but is brought back to teach in Book 6-7

The Bloody Baron

Slytherin House Ghost, and the only one who can control Peeves
